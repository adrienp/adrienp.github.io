Hi, I'm Adrien!

I'm an actor, writer, and comedian living in New York City.

I've been studying and performing improv and sketch comedy at UCB since 2012. My shows have also gone up at The PIT and The Annoyance Theatre.

In 2015, I started TOS Comedy, a Video Sketch Show that has been airing on MNN and BRIC Public Access TV Channels in NYC. My videos have been featured on Mashable and Funny Or Die.

I cowrote a musical comedy show called Meet The CEOs: A Startup Musical, which ran at The PIT in the summer of 2016. I also have a monthly sketch show at The PIT with St. Olaf as part of Boogie Manja.

But way before all that, I found my love for comedy while improvising with Spontaneous Combustion at Rice University starting in 2009, then training with The New Movement Theatre in Austin, TX and Station Theatre in Houston, TX.
